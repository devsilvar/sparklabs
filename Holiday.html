ABOUT OUR SPARK CURRICULUMS

Unlock Your Child's Coding Potential!

Is your child curious about coding and technology? Do you want to give them a head start in developing essential skills for the future? Our FREE 1 month coding course is the perfect opportunity for your child to learn, create, and have fun!

What Will Your Child Learn?

In our 1 Month course, your child will learn the basics of coding and web development. They'll discover how to:

Create interactive web pages

Use problem-solving skills to overcome challenges

Develop critical thinking and logic

Build a project from scratch

Why is Coding Important?

Coding is an essential skill for the future. It helps develop problem-solving skills, critical thinking, and creativity. By learning to code, your child will:

Improve their academic performance

Enhance their career opportunities

Develop a new hobby and passion

Stay ahead of the curve in a rapidly changing world

Our Expert Instructors

Our instructors are experienced coders and educators who are passionate about teaching kids. They'll guide your child through the course, providing support and feedback every step of the way.

What to Expect

1 month of FREE coding lessons

1 hour of instruction per day (Friday and Staurday)

A fun and interactive learning environment

A project to build and showcase at the end of the course

Next Steps

After completing our FREE 1-month course, your child will have the opportunity to enroll in our foundation courses, where they'll dive deeper into the world of coding and web development.
